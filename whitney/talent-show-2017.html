Whitney Talent Show 2017

In January 2017, Whitney High School's Associated Student Body held a design contest in order to select a shirt design that would be used to publicize our annual talent show. My design was chosen and shirts were produced for performers, faculty, and audience members interested in commemorating the event. My design features a storytelling theme, the names of all performers, and the coordinators of the talent show.
