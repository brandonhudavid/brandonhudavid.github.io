TEDxWhitneyHigh

In freshman year of high school, I helped Whitney's Pen on Paper club organize the first ever TEDxWhitneyHigh, a TED Youth event that allows students and faculty to speak their minds and share their ideas. Throughout my high school years, I helped with TEDxWhitneyHigh as Digital Publicity Head, in which I created the program brochures that listed speaker bios and topics, managed the posting of social media graphics to publicize for the event, and designed merchandise for attendees to bring home to commemorate the event.
