Safely

Using HERE's iOS SDK to travel safely.
Product design and application development with the Berkeley Design Office.

SCOPE: collaborative intern project
TIME: 2 months
ROLE: product designer, iOS developer

Near the HERE Berkeley Office

During my summer internship at the HERE Berkeley office, I had the opportunity to work with Vanasa Liu—a product design intern—and the Berkeley Design Office on a location based side project that utilizes HERE's location technology.

Vanasa and I discovered a problem that has an effect on people all around the world: getting to your destination safely. We took a concept from ideation through product design and multiple iterations. And with HERE's extensive location database and readily available APIs, we took this concept all the way to a functional prototype that routes users to their destinations safely.
