ABOUT ME

Hi there! My name is Brandon David, and I am a freshman studying Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at UC Berkeley. Additionally, I am a volleyball libero, a contemporary hip hop fan, a streetwear fashion enthusiast, a Stephen Curry bandwagoner, a Stranger Things follower, a dog lover, and a loud smiler.

My passion for computer science stems from my involvement in high school robotics and web development projects. I also have a strong graphic design background, and am currently an active member of UC Berkeley's leading design agency, Innovative Design. My aspirations range from growing as a full stack developer and facilitating productivity to becoming the game changer for branding and logo design.

Above all, I am a creator and a creative, conscious enough to understand that I still have many aspects of life to explore before I know who I truly am, but confident enough to say that I am constantly exploring and diligently working towards becoming who I want to be.
